‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

Contact your administrator or email support@analytics-dashboard.com with your username and registered email. Our team will send you a password reset link within 24 hours.

Q: I can't access certain dashboards. What should I do?

Dashboard access is role-based. If you believe you should have access but don't, contact your system administrator to review your permissions. Admin users can manage access levels for all users.

Q: How do I export my dashboard data?

Navigate to your saved dashboard, click the "Export" button, and choose your preferred format (HTML, Excel, CSV, or JSON). Export permissions depend on your user role.

Q: Can I request new features?

Absolutely! We welcome feature requests. Email your suggestions to features@analytics-dashboard.com with a detailed description of the functionality you'd like to see.

Q: What browsers are supported?

The Analytics Dashboard works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. For optimal performance, ensure JavaScript is enabled and your browser is up to date.
